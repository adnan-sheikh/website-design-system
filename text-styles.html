Text Styles

These are the text styles that I use very often:

H1: Main page heading

  • font-family: 'Bree Serif', serif
  • font-size: 100px
  • font-weight: 400 (regular)

H2: Sub-heading

  • font-family: 'Alegreya', serif
  • font-size: 30px
  • font-weight: 700 (bold)

p: Paragraph text

  • font-family: 'Alegreya', serif
  • font-size: 20px
  • font-weight: 400 (regular)
